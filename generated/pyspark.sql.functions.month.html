2.3.1.99. pyspark.sql.functions.month¶

pyspark.sql.functions.month(col)[source]¶

Extract the month of a given date as integer.

>>> df = spark.createDataFrame([('2015-04-08',)], ['a'])
>>> df.select(month('a').alias('month')).collect()
[Row(month=4)]

New in version 1.5.
